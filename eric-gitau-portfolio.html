01 — about

From networks to the cloud, and everything that ships in between.

I'm Eric Ngaruiya Gitau, a web developer and cloud engineer based in Kenya. My work sits at the intersection of building things people use — websites, apps, dashboards — and building the infrastructure they run on — provisioned, automated, and monitored the way production systems should be.

I started with networking fundamentals, moved into full-stack development through hands-on project series, and now hold the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner credential alongside practical experience in Terraform, Docker, and serverless AWS architecture.

I learn in public. Most of what I ship lives in open repositories — multi-week challenges, lab series, and full builds — because shipping consistently, in the open, is the fastest way to get better.
